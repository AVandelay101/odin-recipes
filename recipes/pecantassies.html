Pecan Tassies

Description

These Pecan Tassies only have 6 ingredients! They are made of a buttery pie crust and filled with brown sugar and pecan filling. Theyâ€™re the perfect bite-sized treat!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup unsalted cold butter equal to 2 sticks
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups pecans chopped
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped pecans, brown sugar, and eggs. Then set them aside.
  2. In a separate bowl cut the cold butter into the flour until the butter becomes pea-sized. I like to use a pastry cutter for this but you can do it in a food processor as well.
  3. Then combine the cream cheese with the butter and flour. At this point, I just used my hands because the dough begins to form into one piece.
  4. Scoop out 1 tablespoon-sized piece of the dough and form them into balls. Then place them into a greased mini muffin tin.
  5. Using your fingers flatten out each ball so it takes up the shape of the hollow tin. Then fill each pocket with 1 tablespoon of the pecan filling.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for about 20-25 minutes. After the pecan pies cool, you can leave them as they are or drizzle a little bit of melted semisweet chocolate on top. Tip: These can be made in advance and stored in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
